About the Journal

Peercite Journals are inspired by a need among research funders, researchers, librarians, readers, and publishers to sense the quality and safeguard high standards of peer review. Peercite Journals define paradigm scholarly journal publication in the context of publishing rich literature in the concept of innovation and novel gold standard peer review procedures.
